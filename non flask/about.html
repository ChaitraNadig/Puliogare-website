Iyengar’s Home Products [IHP] is serving people from 29 long years with its food product Iyengar puliogare or tamarind rice. Puliogare is an ancient Indian dish. It is prepared by using fire wood in a traditional way. In order to prepare Puliyogare we use tamarind & jaggary paste to that add all masala items fried & mixed to that paste in appropriate manner.


We take meticulous care right from sourcing raw materials, to testing, processing to production and packaging to branding. At every step, quality is ensured with perfection which gives it a higher nutritional value.The organization always aims at providing people with the best quality product at an economic price where anyone can afford to buy it and is very easy to prepare, quick to make besides, it has always maintained its superior quality and taste.


Needless to mention, as it contains highest nutritional value, almost every child and office goers likes it. It has retained the grandma’s homely taste. Most of the schools and college students, including hostels, apart from large number of small and big hotels in Karnataka regularly use our Puliyogare, which is easily accessible to them in the nearest shops and outlets at affordable prices.
